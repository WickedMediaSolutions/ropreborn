CHAT/GOSSIP - Communication Channels

Visual Output:

======================================== COMMUNICATION CHANNELS ======================================== Syntax: gossip - Send to all interested players Syntax: question - Ask a question to all Syntax: answer - Answer the current question Syntax: shout - Shout to all awake players Syntax: yell - Yell to players in your area GOSSIP / . (DOT) Sends your message to all players interested in gossip. Type gossip with no arguments to toggle whether you hear gossip. The . (dot) command is a quick shortcut for gossip.

Visual Output:

======================================== TOP 10 THINGS NEW PLAYERS SHOULD KNOW ======================================== 1. Alignment Defines Your Allies and Enemies Your alignment (good or evil) determines who can attack you. Only opposite alignments can PK each other. Type help alignment to learn more. You cannot change alignment after creation! 2. Use CONSIDER and SCAN Before Every Fight Always consider before attacking - it tells you if you can win. Use scan to see creatures in adjacent rooms. Sometimes consider fails, so try again if you get no response. The command gconsider works for groups. 3. Death Penalties Scale With Level At level 1-20, death penalties are minimal - explore freely!
